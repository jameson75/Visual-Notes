Summary

Predefined Symbols
Visual Notes comes with predefined Flow Chart, Org Chart and UML Symbols. Each symbol has customizable properties so you can create diagrams with your own look and feel.

Prepackaged Bitmaps
Visual Notes comes with prepackaged bitmaps of network symbols. No drawing is required to create visual appealing network models.

Snapped Edges
Question: Do I have to draw an edge over when I move a symbol across the canvas?
Answer: Ofcourse not. In Visual Notes 1.0, edges simply snap to the sides of your symbol Inotherwords, to minimize your efforts, edges move when your symbols move.

Embedded Diagrams
Complex diagrams require complex features. With Visual Notes 1.0, you can embed diagrams into symbols and navigate between them as you please.

Spatial Hierarchies
Use spatial hierarchies to group symbols and control the Z-Order (layering). For example, I usually make my labels children of a symbol they identify so that they always appear infront of the symbol and automatically moves whenever the symbol moves.

Undo/Redo Support
If this is not your first Pocket PC shareware download, you may have noticed the lack of support for both undoing and redoing your actions. That's one feature you won't miss in this application.

Resizable Canvas
Ofcourse we had to include this feature. How could we possibly predict the limitation of your ideas. So make room for your imagination with a resizable canvas area.

Detailed User Help
Although Visual Notes 1.0 is quite intuitive, anyone could use a little help. So we provided ALOT. Visual Notes help files are integrated with the Pocket PC help system to make your life just that much easier.
